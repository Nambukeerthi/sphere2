1. What are the names of all the videos and their corresponding channels?

SELECT c.name,v.videoname FROM channel c JOIN videos v ON c.id=v.channelid;

2. Which channels have the most number of videos, and how many videos do
they have?

SELECT name,videoscount FROM channel ORDER BY videoscount DESC LIMIT 10;

3. What are the top 10 most viewed videos and their respective channels?

SELECT c.name,v.videoname,v.views FROM CHANNEL C LEFT JOIN videos v ON v.channelid=c.id ORDER BY v.views DESC LIMIT 10;

4. How many comments were made on each video, and what are their
corresponding video names?

SELECT v.videoname,v.commentcount,c.text FROM videos v LEFT JOIN comments c ON v.id=c.videosid ORDER BY v.commentcount DESC LIMIT 10; 

5. Which videos have the highest number of likes, and what are their
corresponding channel names?

SELECT c.name,v.likes FROM channel c LEFT JOIN videos v on c.id=v.channelid ORDER BY v.likes DESC LIMIT 10;

6. What is the total number of likes and dislikes for each video, and what are
their corresponding video names?

SELECT c.name,v.videoname,v.likes FROM channel c LEFT JOIN videos v on c.id=v.channelid ORDER BY v.likes DESC LIMIT 10;

7. What is the total number of views for each channel, and what are their
corresponding channel names?

SELECT name,views FROM channel ORDER BY views DESC;

8. What are the names of all the channels that have published videos in the year
2024?
SELECT c.name,v.videoname,v.publisheddate FROM channel c right JOIN videos v ON c.id=v.channelid WHERE YEAR(publisheddate)=2024;
